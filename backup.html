Safari Itinerary

8 Days Across Wild Kenya

Day 1 · 27th December 2026

Welcome to Nairobi

Your adventure begins with a warm meet-and-greet at JKIA Airport. Our representative transfers you to the 4-star Sarova Panafric Hotel in the heart of Nairobi, where you'll settle in for a comfortable evening before the wild journey ahead. Accommodation is on a Bed & Breakfast basis.

JKIA Airport Pickup Sarova Panafric Hotel B&B Accommodation

Day 2 · 28th December 2026

Nairobi → Amboseli

After breakfast, drive 250km (approx. 4 hours) to Amboseli National Park — best known for its free-roaming herds of over 1,600 elephants against the backdrop of Mt. Kilimanjaro. Arrive in time for a hot lunch, then enjoy an afternoon game drive around 4 PM before returning for dinner.

250km Drive Elephant Herds Mt. Kilimanjaro Views Afternoon Game Drive Full Board Lodge

Day 3 · 29th December 2026

Full Day · Amboseli

A flexible day entirely at your pace. Choose Option 1 for an early morning game drive, leisurely midday at the lodge, and an evening drive. Or choose Option 2 for a full-day game drive with a packed lunch, maximising wildlife time on the savannah.

Morning Game Drive Pool & Relaxation Maasai Community Sunset Views

Day 4 · 30th December 2026

Amboseli → Lake Naivasha

Drive 350km (approx. 6 hours) to Lake Naivasha — one of Africa's most renowned freshwater lakes. After a relaxed lunch, embark on a thrilling hippo boat ride and a walking safari at the enchanting Crescent Island Animal Sanctuary, Naivasha's best-kept secret.

Hippo Boat Ride Crescent Island Walk 1,000+ Hippos Bird Watching

Day 5 · 31st December 2026

Naivasha → Lake Nakuru

A short 100km drive to Lake Nakuru National Park. The park is famed for its extraordinary flamingo displays, endangered Rothschild's giraffes, and both black and white rhinos. Afternoon game drive through the rift valley scenery, culminating in a New Year's Eve dinner at the lodge.

Flamingos Black & White Rhinos Rothschild Giraffe Makalia Waterfall New Year's Eve

Day 6 · 1st January 2027

Nakuru → Masai Mara

Ring in the New Year and drive 300km to the legendary Masai Mara — home to the highest concentration of wildlife in Kenya and Africa's greatest safari experience. All the Big Five can be spotted here. Arrive for lunch, rest, then head out for your first Mara afternoon game drive.

The Big Five Big Cats Savannah Landscape Tented Camp

Day 7 · 2nd January 2027

Full Day · Masai Mara

Your final day in the Mara — make it count. Choose from a whole-day game drive, a morning big-cat hunt with afternoon relaxation, or the ultimate option: a hot air balloon ride over the Mara at sunrise (USD 450 pp), followed by game drives and an optional Maasai village visit (USD 30 pp).

Hot Air Balloon (Optional) Maasai Village (Optional) Big Cat Tracking Mara River

Day 8 · 3rd January 2027

Masai Mara → Nairobi

After breakfast, begin the 350km journey back to Nairobi. Spend your final afternoon with optional excursions to the Giraffe Centre or Maasai Market. End your safari in style with an included dinner at the famous Carnivore Restaurant before your transfer to JKIA for your onward flight.

Giraffe Centre (Optional) Maasai Market (Optional) Carnivore Dinner ✓ Included JKIA Transfer
